**Project Duration:** 12 months (ongoing support)  
**Project Type:** Educational Assessment Platform  
**Users:** Teachers and Students (Grades 3-12)  
**Geographic Reach:** Schools across Alberta and Western Canada

#### Description

Developed RCAT (Reading Comprehension Assessment Tool), a comprehensive digital platform designed to effectively diagnose reading comprehension skills and identify specific learning gaps for students in grades 3 through 12. The platform revolutionizes traditional assessment methods by providing teachers with powerful tools to evaluate student reading comprehension through digital screening tests, level tests, and passage-based assessments.

The system enables teachers to schedule assessments for entire classes, specific groups, or individual students through a simple link-sharing mechanism, making it ideal for both in-person and online classroom environments. Teachers gain access to real-time reporting and analytics, allowing them to track student progress, identify struggling readers, and adjust instruction accordingly. The platform supports unlimited students per school and allows assessments to be assigned multiple times throughout the year.

RCAT is curriculum-aligned and provides educators with actionable insights into student comprehension skills including literal understanding, inferential thinking, vocabulary development, and text analysis capabilities.

**Project Duration:** 18 months (ongoing platform enhancement)  
**Project Type:** Multi-Stakeholder Recruitment Platform  
**Users:** International Students, Educational Agents, Universities  
**Geographic Reach:** India, Southeast Asia, Middle East → Canada & USA  
**Scale:** 100+ University Partners, 200+ Recruitment Agents, 10,000+ Students

#### Description

Developed Admicity, a comprehensive international student recruitment portal that bridges the gap between students seeking higher education abroad, educational agents representing students, and North American universities seeking international enrollment. The platform creates a seamless application ecosystem that optimizes the entire recruitment process from initial program search through enrollment.

The platform serves three distinct user groups with dedicated portals: (1) Students who can search programs, submit applications, upload documents, and track admission status; (2) Educational agents who manage multiple student applications, communicate with universities, and access commission tracking; and (3) Universities who review applications, manage admissions decisions, and coordinate with agent networks. The system facilitates thousands of applications annually while maintaining data integrity, security, and compliance with international education regulations.

Admicity has become a critical infrastructure connecting reputable academic institutions across Canada and the United States with qualified international students through a vetted network of professional educational consultants and recruitment agents.

**Multi-Stakeholder Complexity:**

**International Payment Processing:**
- **Challenge:** Handling application fee payments from students in multiple countries with various currencies, payment methods, and banking regulations.
- **Solution:** Integrated with multiple payment gateways supporting international transactions. Implemented currency conversion, multiple payment method support (credit cards, wire transfers, local payment methods), and automated receipt generation. Built reconciliation system for agent commission tracking.

**Document Verification at Scale:**
- **Challenge:** Managing, verifying, and securing thousands of documents (transcripts, diplomas, test scores, passports) uploaded by students and requiring review by agents and universities.
- **Solution:** Built secure document management system with encryption at rest and in transit. Implemented document workflow with status tracking (uploaded, verified, approved, rejected). Created efficient document viewer for admissions staff with zoom, annotation, and comparison capabilities. Integrated automated virus scanning and fraud detection checks.

**University System Integration:**
- **Challenge:** Integrating with diverse university Student Information Systems (SIS) that use different technologies, APIs, and data formats.
- **Solution:** Developed flexible integration layer with configurable data mapping. Built adapters for common SIS platforms (Banner, PeopleSoft, Workday) and generic API/file-based integration for others. Implemented robust error handling, retry logic, and monitoring to ensure data synchronization reliability.

**Data Privacy & Compliance:**
- **Challenge:** Ensuring compliance with multiple data privacy regulations (Canadian PIPEDA, European GDPR, various national laws) while serving international users and sharing data across borders.
- **Solution:** Implemented comprehensive data protection measures including encryption, audit logging, data retention policies, and consent management. Built privacy-by-design features including data minimization, right to access, right to erasure. Conducted privacy impact assessments and maintained detailed data processing records.

**Scalability for Peak Periods:**
- **Challenge:** Handling significant traffic spikes during university application deadlines (Fall and Spring intakes) when thousands of students submit applications simultaneously.
- **Solution:** Designed cloud-native architecture with auto-scaling capabilities. Implemented caching strategies, database query optimization, and asynchronous processing for heavy workloads. Load testing ensured system performance under 5x normal traffic. CDN deployment reduced latency for global users.
